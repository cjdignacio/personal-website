About Jay

A self-described elusive permutation and an advocate of self-satisfying spontaneity, Jay Ignacio’s unpredictability and nonchalant energy has been cited as his artistic and strategic inspiration.

As a financial scholar of an exclusive institution during High School, he learned that recognizing the absurdity of his mundane work-life enabled the potential to escape from its powerlessness. Eventually, he went to work, unearthing optimism from nihilism—treating the latter as a boundless fountain of human insight and truth.

Harnessing the power of one’s intent remains the philosophy that guides his work—a constant meditation recognizing that form must always follow function, and that a function must always challenge itself to be as certain and as absolute as it can possibly be.

While he knows he has a long way to go in terms of mastering the art of leveraging one's intent, he acknowledges that recognizing it is the first step to take in a journey toward becoming a bona fide Strategist and Creative—one he has already begun.
